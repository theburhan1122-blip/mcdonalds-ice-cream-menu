Introduction

Craving something cold and creamy after your meal but unsure what McDonald's offers? You're not alone. Many people walk past the dessert counter simply because they don't know the full range of frozen treats available. That quick craving often goes unsatisfied because the menu boards move so fast, or the ice cream machine seems like a mystery.

Good news — the McDonald's ice cream menu has way more options than you might expect. From rich sundaes to loaded McFlurries, there's something for every sweet tooth. This guide breaks down every item, price, and calorie count so you can pick your perfect treat without the guesswork. Whether you are looking for a budget-friendly cone or a decadent McFlurry loaded with toppings, we have mapped it all out for you.

What's on the McDonald's Dessert Menu?

McDonald's serves a solid lineup of frozen desserts and baked goods. You'll find sundaes in different flavors, McFlurries with popular candy mix-ins, frozen drinks, pies, and fresh-baked cookies. Whether you want something light or indulgent, the McD dessert menu with prices below has you covered. It's a mix of nostalgic classics that have been around for decades and new, limited-time innovations that keep fans coming back.

Top 6 McDonald's Ice Cream Treats
You Must Try

Fan Fave

1. OREO McFlurry

This one's a fan favorite for a reason. Creamy vanilla soft serve blended with crushed OREO cookie pieces. It's rich, crunchy, and hits every time.

Why it works:

Perfect ratio of cookie crunch to smooth cream.

2. Hot Fudge Sundae

Warm chocolate fudge drizzled over cold vanilla ice cream — simple but perfect. Great for anyone who loves that hot-and-cold contrast in every bite.

Classic Choice:

The temperature contrast is the key experience here.

3. M&M McFlurry

Colorful candy-coated chocolates mixed into smooth soft serve. It's fun, sweet, and gives you that satisfying crunch.

Texture Profile:

Hard candy shell meets soft serve smoothness.

4. Caramel Sundae

Buttery caramel sauce poured generously over vanilla ice cream. The perfect sweet finish after enjoying your favorite burger—if you prefer something sweeter and smoother to end your meal, this is your pick.

Sweet Tooth Alert:

The sweetest option on the menu.

Premium

5. KIT KAT Banana Split McFlurry

A twist on the classic banana split. Vanilla ice cream loaded with KIT KAT pieces and banana flavor. It feels fancy without the fancy price.

Flavor Twist:

Combines fruity notes with chocolate wafer crunch.

Budget Pick

6. Plain Sundae

Sometimes you just want pure, no-fuss vanilla soft serve. Clean taste, creamy texture, and the cheapest sundae option on the menu.

Purist Choice:

Best way to taste the actual quality of the dairy.

Did You Know?

The McDonald's soft serve machine is actually a marvel of engineering, designed to pump air into the mixture (overrun) to give it that signature light texture. Without this process, the ice cream would be a solid block of milk ice!

Pro Tip: If you are ordering a McFlurry and find the toppings are all at the top, don't be afraid to ask for it to be mixed a bit longer, or simply give it a good stir yourself before digging in. The distribution of candy pieces is key to the perfect bite.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Does McDonald's have real ice cream?
Yes, McDonald's uses reduced-fat vanilla soft serve for sundaes and McFlurries. It is made primarily from milk, sugar, and cream, qualifying it as a dairy dessert.
2. What's the cheapest ice cream at McDonald's?
The Plain Sundae at $3.89 is the most affordable option, offering a budget-friendly way to enjoy their signature soft serve without extra toppings.
3. Are McFlurries made fresh?
Yes, each McFlurry is blended fresh when you order. The crew member dispenses the soft serve, adds the toppings, and uses the machine's spindle to mix it right in the cup.
4. Does McDonald's sell milkshakes?
Yes, milkshakes are available separately on the drinks menu in flavors like Chocolate, Vanilla, and Strawberry.
5. Can I customize my McFlurry?
Toppings are fixed for specific menu items, but you can ask for extra or less mix-ins. Some locations may allow you to mix sauces (like adding hot fudge to an OREO McFlurry) for a small extra charge.
6. Are McDonald's sundaes gluten-free?
The plain sundae is gluten-free, but toppings may contain gluten (especially the OREO crumbs or KitKat pieces). Always check the official allergen guide if you have severe sensitivity due to cross-contamination risks.
7. How many calories in a Hot Fudge Sundae?
Around 330 calories per serving. It is considered a moderate treat compared to the larger McFlurries which can exceed 500 calories.
8. What time does McDonald's stop serving ice cream?
Ice cream is available all day, but machine availability may vary. The machines undergo a daily heat treatment cycle (usually late at night or early morning) during which ice cream cannot be served.
9. Is the soft serve dairy-free?
No, McDonald's soft serve contains dairy (milk and cream). It is not suitable for vegans or those with lactose intolerance.
10. Can I order desserts through the app?
Yes, all desserts are available for mobile ordering and delivery. Using the app can sometimes score you exclusive deals, like a $1 cone or discounted sundaes.

That wraps up everything you need to know about the McDonald's ice cream menu. Next time you're at the counter, you'll know exactly what to order!
